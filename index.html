Cheap commerce options

The sensible paths, ordered by effort and cost.

Static site + Etsy checkout

Lowest cost, easiest upkeep, fastest launch. Best while products and demand are still being proven.

WordPress + WooCommerce

Cheap to host and mature, but needs updates, backups, security care and plugin discipline.

Medusa or Saleor

Open-source and powerful, but more engineering-heavy. Best when custom checkout is genuinely needed.

Quick answers

Can customers buy directly here?

For launch, the site sends customers to Etsy for checkout. Direct checkout can be added later.

Will the domain work with this?

Yes. The site can be deployed to low-cost static hosting and pointed at littlewallwonders.co.uk.

Can product pages be added?

Yes. The current structure is ready for individual collection or product pages when listings are final.
