Our story

Little Wall Wonders began after decorating our own toddler’s bedroom with wall decals and seeing just how much joy they brought to the space.

Like most toddlers, she absolutely loves stickers — whether they’re covering laptops, furniture or occasionally our foreheads — and we loved how colourful, creative and personal her room became.

It sparked the idea to try creating our own collections combining Emma’s design background with Tom’s technical and web experience.

Designed & printed at home

Every decal and print is designed, prepared and produced by us from our home studio in the UK.

Emma creates the artwork and illustrations while Tom handles the production, print setup, website and technical side of the business.

We wanted to build something that felt creative, playful and genuinely personal from start to finish.

Made for playful little spaces

Our collections are designed for nurseries, bedrooms and playrooms filled with imagination, colour and personality.

We focus on creating decals and prints that feel playful and modern while still fitting beautifully into family homes.

Most of all, we want to help create spaces children genuinely love spending time in.
