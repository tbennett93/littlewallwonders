FAQs

You may have questions about our decals and prints, which we aim to answer here. For anything else, please feel free to contact us

What are your wall decals made from?

Our decals are printed on a premium fabric material with a soft matte finish that feels more premium than traditional shiny vinyl.

Are the decals removable?

Yes — our decals are removable and repositionable on suitable smooth painted surfaces.

Will they damage my walls?

Our decals are designed to remove cleanly from suitable surfaces. We always recommend testing first on delicate, freshly painted or poorly bonded paint.

Can I reposition the decals?

Yes. The fabric material allows you to peel and reposition the decals while decorating your space.

What surfaces work best?

Smooth painted walls work best. Heavily textured walls or flaky paint may affect adhesion.

Do prints come framed?

No — frames are not included unless otherwise stated in the product listing.

Where do you ship?

We currently ship across the UK through our Etsy shop.

How long does delivery take?

Estimated delivery times are shown on Etsy before checkout and may vary depending on order volume.
